# Constants
TrackPlayer statuses:
* 0 . STATE_NONE
* 1 . STATE_STOPPED
* 2 . STATE_PAUSED
* 2 . STATE_READY
* 3 . STATE_PLAYING
* 6 . STATE_BUFFERING
* 8 . STATE_CONNECTING
